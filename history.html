📅 Our Journey

2015

🎉 Branch was Started

Our IEEE student branch was officially started at College of Engineering Kidangoor. We started with just a small group of students who were passionate about technology.

2016

🚀 First Major Event

We conducted our first big technical event which was attended by over 100 students. That was a huge deal for us back then!

2018

🏆 First Award

We received our first branch level award from the IEEE Kerala section. This motivated us a lot to keep going and do better.

2020

💻 Went Online

During the pandemic we shifted all our events online. We actually reached more students this way and learned a lot about doing virtual events.

2022

⚡ New Societies Formed

We started new technical societies under our branch to focus on specific areas like AI, robotics and communication systems.

2024

🌟 Most Active Branch Award

We were recognized as one of the most active branches in the Kerala section. We conducted 15+ events that year alone!

2026

🔥 Present Day

Today our branch is stronger than ever with more members, more events and more opportunities for students at CEK to grow!
